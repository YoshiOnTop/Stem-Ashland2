Welcome to Ashland STEM Club

Ashland STEM is a locally run club held on Saturdays at 11:00am during the summer, from July through August, at the local library in the recreation room. Parents are welcome to stay.

Our STEM club aims to provide an engaging and educational experience for kids (K-5) interested in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
